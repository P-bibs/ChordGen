How do I use This?

To generate a new progression, click the green button on the left. To play it, click the green button on the right. This will play each chord in the progression for two seconds each. If you want to stop playback early, press the red button.

While the progression is playing, the currently playing chord will be displayed on the left, with its chordal members above. The next chord to be played will be displayed on the right.
